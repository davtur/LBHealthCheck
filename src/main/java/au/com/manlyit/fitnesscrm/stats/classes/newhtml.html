Each trainer takes 1-2 classes per week, and those sessions rely on your attendance! As we draw closer to the colder months, we want to ensure class numbers don’t drop!

class durations:

If you have attended the 6am classes this week, your trainers would have explained we are trialling 45-minute session times in April. This is due to the approaching colder and darker months, and considers our new and existing corporate members. It also means class times begin bang on 6am!
this strategy allows us to work towards eradicating slow starts, efficiently running our workout plans and, with colder mornings, encourages the cool down component (stretching) to occur at home - retaining the warmth you just worked hard to create! on Saturday’s it allows us to all stay together and have a coffee close by :)

cooler conditions:

There is already snow on the mountains and it’s about to get colder over the next few weeks! Working out in the cold is good for your health especially when matched with:

  • A good sleep the night before
  • Eating a good meal
  • Wearing the correct clothes!
during these times it's beneficial to wear clothing made from good quality material that responds well to sweat in cold weather - our local store - Patagonia, has a great selection in shop and online.
